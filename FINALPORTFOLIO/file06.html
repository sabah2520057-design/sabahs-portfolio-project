The Imprint

This installation contrasts a large human footprint,cast from my own whcich represents a dominating a textured sand base with fading carvings of extinct animal prints, symbolizing humanity's overwhelming impact and species loss. Crafted from air-dry clay, sand, and layered paint in muted tones, its tactile textures evoke environmental degradation. It prompts reflection on our ecological footprint and accountability in coexisting with nature

Duration : 2 weeks Type : Individual Skills Developed/ Used : Conceptualization ; carving ; painting ; material exploration; Installation design ; Symbolisim.

Dali Mural

This collaborative mural explores Salvador Dalí’s surrealist style, blending his portrait with symbolic elements from Meditative Rose and The Persistence of Memory. Vibrant colors, layered compositions, and dreamlike details capture the works' emotional depth. From research and sketches to large-scale execution with mentorship, the process refined color layering, composition, and teamwork, creating a public art tribute to abstraction and portraiture.

Duration: 3 Days Project Type: Group Tools Used: Acrylic Paints, Brushes, Wall Surface, Sketching Materials
