Axomiya Xuwad

Axomiya Xuwad (“Assamese Flavours”) is an interactive menu and branding project inspired by the traditional Assamese hand fan and Jaapi. Designed in a folding, circular fan format, the menu celebrates Assam’s food culture through storytelling, texture, and form which was crafted using actual rattan to reflect authentic local craftsmanship.Illustrated elements like rice, red chilies, and spices nod to Assamese cuisine, while a warm palette of Flush Mahogany and Indian Khaki evokes an earthy, rooted tone. Each layer features textile-inspired patterns, and a QR code adds a digital touchpoint

Duration - 5 days Project type - Individual Skills developed/used: Cultural Research;Branding & Identity;Logo Design;Typography for Print;Menu Layout;Packaging Mockup Design;Color Palette Development;Real-world Applications

Scribble & Mark Design Solutions

This visiting card for Scribble & Mark Design Solutions uses a natural kraft paper background paired with hand-drawn wave motifs, reflecting creativity and organic design. The minimalist layout highlights the brand’s logo establishing professionalism and clarity. The design balances artistry with readability, using elegant serif typography to convey sophistication. The overall concept shows the brand as approachable yet refined, appealing to clients seeking bespoke design solutions. The tactile, eco-friendly aesthetic reinforces the studio’s commitment to thoughtful, sustainable creativity, making a memorable first impression.

Duration : 3 days Type : Individual Skills Developed /Used : Branding ,Typography ; Illustration ; Brand Identity.
