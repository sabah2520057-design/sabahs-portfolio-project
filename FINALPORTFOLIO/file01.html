Sparky’s Adventures

Created a children’s picture book for the ages of 4 & 5, which is culturally rich. It introduces young readers to the famous cities and landmarks of India using a mascot. The project merges educational design with play, involving puppets and “guess what” clues, to enhance storytelling into an active experience.

Duration: 1.5 weeks
Type: Individual Project
Tools: Adobe Fresco, Photoshop, InDesign
Skills: Visual narratives, Character design, Layout formatting, Typography;Interactive elements

La Derniere Note

Designed a visually immersive and emotionally layered set of theatrical posters for an original play concept, La Dernière Note. Rooted in 1980s Parisian opera culture, the project reimagines traditional mystery storytelling through the lens of a graphic designer.The posters unravel a suspenseful narrative in three acts each design revealing symbolic clues tied to a murder on stage.I used textured illustration, poetic typography, and metaphor-driven imagery (like a chipped teacup or vanishing spotlight) to spark intrigue and invite deeper viewer engagement.

Duration : 2.5 weeks Type : Individual Project Tools Used : Adobe Fresco, Photoshop and InDesign Skills Developed/Used:Narrative Illustration;Symbolism & Metaphor;Visual Storytelling;Composition & Layout;Color Psychology;Era-specific Design Research
