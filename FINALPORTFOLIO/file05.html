Font Poster

This poster celebrates the sans-serif typeface while emphasizing its clean,classic and versatile nature. It has been like a movie marquee which was widely used in the 80's and to show its popularity. The background is subtle and is textured adding depth and warmth to the poster. A neutral but bold color palette has been followed to make the poster educatinal yet visually entertaining as our eyes flow through it.

Playing Cards

This typographic poster tribute to Nirvana reimagines their iconic smiley face logo using lyrics from hit songs, doubling as a vinyl sleeve concept. Every word conveys the band's raw chaos, rage, numbness, clarity, distortion, rebellion, and vulnerability through a grunge aesthetic. Drawn to text-only emotional intensity, the design evokes disillusionment and defiance, ready to spin on a turntable.

Duration: 1 week Project type: Individual Skills: Illustration, Typography, Layout Design, Packaging Design
