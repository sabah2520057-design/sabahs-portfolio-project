About Me

Hello! I'm Sabah Mamajiwala, an aspiring communication designer based in Mumbai. I'm passionate about the power of emotions and storytelling to create truly impactful narratives. My journey began with a childhood fascination for recreating cartoons, an early inspiration that sparked my love for design's creative potential. Today, I channel that passion into crafting meaningful stories and experiences. Outside of design, I find joy in dancing, reading, and caring for animals. Feel free to explore my portfolio and see how I bring stories to life.
